Class Taught by PMI Registered Education Provider (R.E.P.)

All courses offered by PMTraining are instructed by PMI Registered Education Providers (R.E.P.s). All class curriculums offered by our course providers have been assessed by PMI, and are preapproved for contact hours as well as PDUs. All classes in our PMP course schedule are valid for 35 PMI Contact Hours and PDU Credits.

Focused PMP Certification Curriculum

Each class will intensively cover all areas of the latest PMBOK Guide®, and will instill a comprehensive knowledge of all ten PMBOK Guide® Knowledge Areas, five basic process groups, the Project Framework; and Professional and Social Responsibility. Students will leave with a solid foundation of Project Management, ready to become a PMP.

Extensive Study Tools Included with Every Class

Students will be provided with the most effective study tools currently available on the market, including offline exam preparation tools, a comprehensive class manual, and access to realistic exam simulation tools. Please note: Due to copyright restrictions, students will need to acquire their own version of the PMBOK Guide®.

PMTraining Online Membership Included

Master the pace, subject matter, and difficulty of the PMP exam with a free membership to PMTraining's online exam practice system (a $59.00 value). You will have access to over 1,000 PMP practice exam questions, answers, and detailed explanations; including detailed score reports which save you time by pinpointing PMBOK Guide® knowledge areas which require more study.

Contact hours and PDUs Included with Course

All classes are officially recognized by the Project Management Institute (PMI) and include 35 Contact Hours (amount required to take the PMP exam). In addition, each class includes PDUs for use with your future PMP certification.

One-on-one QA session with Instructor

Similar to in-class courses, students will be encouraged to engage with the instructor. As an added bonus, all students will be allotted time for "one-on-one" sessions with their instructor, to answer questions and clarify concepts.

PMI Application and Audit Assistance

Applying for the PMP exam is a breeze with step-by-step application assistance. Instructions and guidance is provided to all students who require it. Students selected by PMI for audit will be provided with additional assistance.

Email Access to Your PMP Instructor

We understand that students have questions outside of class hours. All students will be provided with direct email access to their instructor, in the event they have additional questions or feedback on PMP-related topics.

We Guarantee your PMP

With a near-perfect 97%+ first time pass rate, we guarantee you will pass the PMP exam and become certified. If you do not pass the PMP exam the first time, you will be provided with free instructor assistance, and free course re-enrollment without limit. Review our complete no-nonsense exam pass guarantee.

The Best Classes at the Lowest Prices

All online classes offered through PMTraining must adhere to an exhaustive list of quality and pass rate guidelines. In addition, our extensive technology infrastructure allows us to offer classes at the lowest prices in the industry. Students can be sure they are receiving an incredible value.
